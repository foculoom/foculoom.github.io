Accessibility

Accessibility matters here.

Foculoom aims to make this website and its products easier to read, navigate, and use for more people from the start.

Last updated: March 16, 2026.

Current website support

  • Semantic landmarks and heading structure.
  • A skip-to-content link on every page.
  • Visible focus states for keyboard navigation.
  • Readable contrast, scalable text, and touch-friendly controls.
  • Reduced-motion support for in-page navigation and interface transitions.

Product direction

Foculoom products are built toward clear structure, readable contrast, larger text, keyboard-friendly interaction where it fits, and reduced-motion-friendly behavior where appropriate. Product-specific accessibility details will appear with each product as it becomes available.

Report an issue

  • Email support@foculoom.com with Accessibility in the subject line.
  • Include the page or feature, your browser or app, your device, and your operating system version.
  • If assistive technology is involved, include that too along with the steps needed to reproduce the issue.
  • If a workaround exists, include it.

What this site avoids

  • No autoplay media or surprise audio.
  • No popups, slide-out panels, or motion-heavy effects on the public site.
  • No required account just to read public company, product, support, or policy pages.

How updates are checked

This site is checked locally for page titles, descriptions, headings, skip links, landmarks, manifest data, and broken local links before updates go live.

Accessibility remains ongoing work, and feedback still matters because automated checks do not catch everything.
