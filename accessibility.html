Accessibility

Accessibility is part of the direction from the start.

This page describes the current accessibility posture of the public Foculoom website and the accessibility principles guiding Focus & Tasks.

Website choices in this version

  • Semantic HTML structure and visible focus states.
  • Readable contrast and scalable text layout.
  • No autoplay media and no motion-heavy interactions.
  • Navigation that works without a pointer device.

Product direction

The broader Foculoom direction includes accessibility-first product decisions such as VoiceOver support, larger-text support, sufficient contrast, and reduced-motion-friendly interactions where appropriate.

How to get accessibility help

  • Email support@foculoom.com with Accessibility in the subject line.
  • Include the page or app area, device, operating system version, browser or app version, and the steps needed to reproduce the issue.
  • Foculoom LLC's public business contact details remain available in the footer and on the support page.

What this page is for

This page stays public as the current accessibility information surface for the website and the publicly described launch direction of Focus & Tasks. It is intended to help users understand the current website posture, the product accessibility priorities already described publicly, and how to contact Foculoom if something is not working as expected.
