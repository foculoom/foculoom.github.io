BubblePop: Focus Game for Kids

Privacy Policy

BubblePop: Focus Game for Kids collects no data. The game runs entirely on your device, requires no account, shows no ads, and makes no in-app purchases. This policy explains that commitment.

Effective date: March 21, 2026.
Published by: Foculoom LLC (DBA Foculoom).

No data collected

  • BubblePop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • No name, email address, device identifier, or usage information is sent to Foculoom or any third party.
  • Apple's App Store privacy nutrition label for BubblePop is: No data collected.

No ads

  • BubblePop contains no advertisements of any kind.
  • No ad network SDKs are included.
  • No cross-app tracking or interest-based advertising occurs.

No in-app purchases

  • BubblePop is a single purchase. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or premium upgrades.
  • All game content is available from the start. Nothing is locked behind a paywall.

No account required

  • BubblePop does not require or offer account creation.
  • No sign-in, no profile, no cloud sync.
  • Game progress is stored locally on your device only.
Children's Privacy

COPPA compliance

BubblePop is designed for children ages 5–12. It is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

What COPPA requires

  • No collection of personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • No behavioral advertising directed at children.
  • No tracking of children's online activities across apps or websites.

How BubblePop complies

  • No personal information is collected — there is nothing to consent to.
  • No advertising of any kind is present.
  • No tracking technologies, SDKs, or analytics are embedded in the app.
  • The app does not connect to the internet during gameplay.

What stays on your device

  • Game progress and high scores are stored locally on the device running the app.
  • No game data is uploaded to Foculoom or any third-party server.
  • Removing the app from a device removes its local data, subject to your device or iCloud backup settings.

When Foculoom may receive information

  • If a parent or guardian contacts support by email, the message may include the details they choose to share.
  • Foculoom does not receive any information from the app itself during normal gameplay.
  • Apple may process transaction and account information when the app is purchased through the App Store, under Apple's privacy policy.
