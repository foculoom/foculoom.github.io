Support

Questions about BubblePop? Email support@foculoom.com. We read every message and respond to parents and guardians directly.

Email support
Include your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue when possible. For billing and refund questions, include your purchase date.
Last updated: March 21, 2026.

Game questions

  • How to play Tap bubbles before they disappear. Bubbles appear at different speeds — the game adapts to help your child stay focused without frustration.
  • No levels locked All content is available from the start. Nothing requires a purchase to unlock.
  • Progress not saving Game progress is stored locally on your device. If progress was lost, it may have been cleared with a device restore or app reinstall.

Technical issues

  • App crashes or freezes Restart the app first. If the issue continues, restart your device and try again. Email support if the problem persists.
  • Performance issues BubblePop is designed to run on older devices. If you notice slowness, closing other apps and restarting may help.
  • What to include in your email Device model (e.g. iPhone 13), iOS version, app version (found in the App Store), and the steps that led to the issue.

Billing and refunds

  • Purchases are made through Apple BubblePop is sold through the App Store. Billing, receipts, and refunds are handled by Apple.
  • Request a refund from Apple Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund from Apple directly.
  • Questions for Foculoom If you need help beyond the Apple refund process, email billing@foculoom.com with your purchase date and a description of the issue.

Privacy questions

  • No data is collected BubblePop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. See the BubblePop privacy policy for full details.
  • No ads, no purchases The game contains no advertising and no in-app purchases of any kind.
  • Privacy requests Email support@foculoom.com with "BubblePop privacy" in the subject line.

About BubblePop

  • A calm focus game for kids ages 5–12.
  • Pop bubbles before they vanish — simple mechanics, no fail states, no timers.
  • Designed for ADHD focus training and attention practice.
  • No ads. No in-app purchases. No accounts. No data collected.
  • Available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
