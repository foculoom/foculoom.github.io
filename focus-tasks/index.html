Flagship product

Plan Today. Do One Thing.

Foculoom: Focus & Tasks is a calm personal planner in development for adults with desk-based work who plan on Mac and execute on iPhone. Capture what is on your mind, choose what matters today, and focus on one task at a time - without clutter, accounts, or extra setup.

This page reflects the current product direction, not a final launch contract. The preview sections below are representative and may change before release.
Launch direction

What the flagship is trying to solve

Mainstream productivity apps can feel cluttered, collaborative, or high-stimulation. Focus & Tasks is being shaped as a calmer alternative for personal planning on Apple devices.

  • General-audience product direction.
  • Single-user planning, not team collaboration.
  • Mac plus iPhone direction for the first launch loop.
  • Productivity framing, not wellness or therapy framing.
Deliberately out of scope

What the first launch is not

  • No AI assistant or auto-planning promise.
  • No shared lists, comments, or team workflows.
  • No caregiver dashboard, classroom mode, or child-directed flagship positioning.
  • No exact launch pricing announced yet.

Planned free core

Current launch shape

The current business direction is a generous free core plus an optional premium tier named Focus & Tasks Plus. Exact pricing is intentionally not published here yet.

Free core

Start with the calm essentials

  • Brain dump and quick capture inbox.
  • Today view with Most Important Task style prioritization.
  • Basic tasks with sub-steps and progress.
  • One-task focus mode with a standard Pomodoro timer.
  • Basic widgets plus core reminders and notifications.
  • Local-first use on Mac and iPhone without a required Foculoom account.
Focus & Tasks Plus

Add more structure when you need it

  • Recurring tasks and reusable routines.
  • Calendar and Reminders integration.
  • Richer organization through projects, areas, and lists for heavier use.
  • Custom focus timer presets and advanced notification controls.
  • Focus history, productivity stats, and advanced widgets.
