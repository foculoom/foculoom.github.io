Made for daily planning

From a crowded list to a clear next step.

Focus & Tasks is for adults with desk-based work who want a calmer way to plan the day.

  • Capture what is on your mind without slowing down.
  • Pick a short list of priorities for today.
  • Break bigger tasks into steps that are easier to start.
  • Move into a simple focus mode when it is time to do the work.
Why it feels calmer

Less overhead, more momentum.

  • No account required.
  • No team features or comment threads getting in the way.
  • Simple structure that makes the next step easier to spot.
  • Mac and iPhone views built around clarity instead of clutter.
How it works

A simpler loop for daily work.

Focus & Tasks revolves around four simple moves: capture, choose, break down, and focus.

1

Capture what is on your mind

Start with fast inbox capture so ideas and obligations stop competing for mental space.

Inbox
  • Prepare today's MITs
  • Review the next draft
  • Plan the next work block
2

Choose what matters today

Turn the inbox into a calmer Today view built around your next important tasks.

Today / MIT
  • Finish the first draft
  • Review the follow-up notes
  • Prepare the final pass
3

Break larger work into steps

Use task breakdown when a project feels too big to start in a single leap.

Task breakdown
  • Write the opening section
  • Refine the supporting points
  • Review the final draft
4

Focus on one thing at a time

Switch into a single-task view with a simple timer when it is time to stop organizing and start doing.

One-task focus
  • 25:00 - Write the opening section
  • Next: review the draft
Built on familiar planning patterns

Grounded in ways people already try to manage a day.

Focus & Tasks brings together familiar ideas: quick capture, Most Important Task planning, task breakdown, and time-boxed focus.

Clear priorities

Today and MIT planning keep the day's priorities small enough to act on, instead of turning planning into another source of pressure.

Smaller starting steps

Task breakdown and short focus sessions help turn larger work into something easier to begin and easier to finish.

Why this approach matters

Planning software should lower friction, not create more of it.

Focus & Tasks is designed to make the next step easier to see and easier to start. That matters for people juggling busy days, limited time, or task systems that already ask for too much attention.

What's included

A strong everyday core, with optional depth when you want more structure.

The main app is designed to feel complete on its own. Focus & Tasks Plus is for people who want recurring planning, calendar context, and a richer system around the same calm workflow.

Core features

Everything you need for daily planning

The core experience is meant to be useful from day one, without pressure to upgrade just to get the basics right.

  • Quick capture for everything on your mind.
  • Today and MIT prioritization.
  • Task breakdown into smaller steps.
  • One-task focus with a standard Pomodoro timer.
  • Basic widgets for glanceable planning.
  • Local-first use on Mac and iPhone.
  • No account required.
Focus & Tasks Plus

Extra structure for routines, timing, and deeper organization

Plus is for people who want repeating tasks, time-aware planning, and more room to shape the app around how they work.

  • Recurring routines and repeating tasks.
  • Calendar integration for time-aware planning.
  • Reminders integration.
  • Richer organization for larger areas of work.
  • Custom focus presets for different work sessions.
  • History and stats to reflect on your pace over time.
  • Advanced widgets.
