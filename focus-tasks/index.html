First app coming soon

Plan Today. Do One Thing.

Foculoom: Focus & Tasks is a calm personal planner for adults with desk-based work who plan on Mac and execute on iPhone. Capture tasks fast, choose what matters today, and focus on one step at a time - without clutter, collaboration overhead, or a required account.

See the core loop See the launch shape
Focus & Tasks is the first public Foculoom release being prepared for the App Store. It is a productivity app, not a medical or mental-health service.
Launch direction

What the flagship is trying to solve

Mainstream productivity apps can feel cluttered, collaborative, or high-stimulation. Focus & Tasks is being shaped as a calmer alternative for personal planning on Apple devices.

  • General-audience product direction.
  • Single-user planning, not team collaboration.
  • Mac plus iPhone for the first launch loop.
  • Productivity framing, not wellness or therapy framing.
Deliberately out of scope

What the first release is not

  • No medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or mental-health guidance.
  • No AI assistant or automatic-planning promise.
  • No shared lists, comments, or team workflows.
  • No caregiver dashboard, classroom mode, or child-directed flagship positioning.
  • No exact launch pricing announced yet.
How the first release works

A simpler loop for daily work

The first public version is designed around four low-friction steps: capture, choose, break down, and focus.

1

Capture what is on your mind

Start with fast inbox capture so ideas and obligations stop competing for mental space.

Inbox
  • Prepare today's MITs
  • Review the next draft
  • Plan the next work block
2

Choose what matters today

Turn the inbox into a calmer Today view built around your next important tasks.

Today / MIT
  • Finish the first draft
  • Review the follow-up notes
  • Prepare the final pass
3

Break larger work into steps

Use task breakdown when a project feels too big to start in a single leap.

Task breakdown
  • Write the opening section
  • Refine the supporting points
  • Review the final draft
4

Focus on one thing at a time

Switch into a single-task view with a simple timer when it is time to stop organizing and start doing.

One-task focus
  • 25:00 - Write the opening section
  • Next: review the draft
Current launch shape

Start with the calm essentials

The current business direction is a generous free core plus an optional premium tier named Focus & Tasks Plus. Exact pricing is intentionally held until the App Store listing is finalized.

Free core

Daily planning without extra overhead

  • Quick capture for everything on your mind.
  • Today and MIT prioritization.
  • Task breakdown into smaller steps.
  • One-task focus with a standard Pomodoro timer.
  • Basic widgets.
  • Local-first use on Mac and iPhone.
  • No account required.
Focus & Tasks Plus

Add more structure when you need it

  • Recurring routines.
  • Calendar integration.
  • Reminders integration.
  • Richer organization for heavier planning.
  • Custom focus presets.
  • History and stats.
  • Advanced widgets.
