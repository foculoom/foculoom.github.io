Pre-launch

Apps and games for neurodivergent minds.

Foculoom is building calm, accessible tools that help people think, focus, and thrive. The first product in development is Foculoom: Focus & Tasks, a local-first productivity app for adults with desk-based work who plan on Mac and execute on iPhone.

This site is intentionally narrow while the first app is still in development. Launch timing, pricing, and app-specific policies will be published closer to release.
Current direction

Flagship-first, calm, and low-clutter.

Foculoom is pre-revenue and pre-launch. The current product plan is to launch a flagship productivity app first, then expand only after that loop is working.

What the first launch is optimizing for

  • A calm, low-distraction planning experience.
  • Single-user personal productivity on Apple platforms.
  • A generous free core with an optional premium upgrade for heavier use.
  • Public messaging that stays accurate while the product is still evolving.

What this site will avoid

  • Unconfirmed product pages or feature promises.
  • Exact launch pricing before it is locked.
  • Medical, therapy, or wellness claims.
  • AI-assistant, collaboration, or caregiver-dashboard messaging for the flagship.

Principles

How Foculoom is approaching launch

Calm by default

Interfaces should feel focused, clear, and low-friction rather than crowded or high-stimulation.

Accessible and readable

Accessibility is part of the direction from the start, including readable structure, strong contrast, and reduced-motion-friendly choices.

Transparent about what is still changing

This site only publishes what the current business documents can already support. Anything else waits.
