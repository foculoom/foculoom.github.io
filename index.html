What guides the work

Mission first, flagship first.

Foculoom's broader mission is calm, accessible software. The public site stays grounded in the first real release and the first real customer problem.

What Foculoom is optimizing for

  • Calm, readable interfaces with less noise and less setup overhead.
  • Accessible defaults from the start, including clear structure and strong contrast.
  • Local-first personal planning instead of social feeds, team workspaces, or collaboration pressure.
  • Honest public messaging that matches the real launch scope.

What the first release keeps avoiding

  • Medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, therapy, or wellness claims.
  • AI assistant or automatic-planning promises.
  • Team collaboration, classroom, or caregiver-dashboard positioning.
  • Exact pricing before the App Store listing is finalized.
What is public now

Start with Focus & Tasks.

Foculoom's first public product story is intentionally concentrated on the flagship launch. Additional apps may follow later, but this site is built around the first release that is actively being prepared for the App Store.
