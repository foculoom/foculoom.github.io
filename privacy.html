Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Foculoom LLC (DBA Foculoom) receives through foculoom.com and support email, how that information is used, and what choices you have.

Effective date: March 12, 2026.
Last reviewed: March 16, 2026.

What this policy covers

  • The Foculoom website at foculoom.com.
  • Emails sent to support@foculoom.com, billing@foculoom.com, or hello@foculoom.com.
  • Information connected with the Focus & Tasks page and other product pages on this site.

What this policy does not cover

  • Third-party sites, app stores, or payment systems after you leave foculoom.com.
  • Device-level settings or privacy controls managed by Apple or other platforms.
  • Any future product-specific data flow that is described separately in a product privacy notice.

Website information

Foculoom does not use client-side advertising, analytics, or social tracking scripts on this website. Like most websites, the hosting and delivery services that keep the site online may process routine technical information such as IP address, browser details, and request information needed to deliver pages securely.

Support information

If you email Foculoom, the message may include your email address, the details you choose to send, screenshots, device information, purchase details, and any other information relevant to your question. That information is used to respond, troubleshoot, keep support records, and improve the quality of the website and products.

Product information

If a Foculoom app or game later includes product-specific privacy details beyond this page, the relevant product page or listing will link to that information.

Categories of information

  • Contact information, such as your email address.
  • Support details, such as screenshots, device information, app version information, purchase details, and the issue you report.
  • Routine technical request information used to deliver and protect the website.

How information is used

  • To deliver and secure the website.
  • To respond to support, billing, privacy, and accessibility questions.
  • To troubleshoot reported issues.
  • To meet legal obligations when required.

Sharing and selling

  • Foculoom does not sell personal information collected through this website.
  • Foculoom does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Information may be handled by service providers that support website hosting, email, or routine operations.
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or needed to address fraud, abuse, or safety concerns.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether to contact Foculoom by email.
  • You can stop using the website at any time.
  • If a future app asks for device permissions, you can manage those permissions in your device settings.

Access, correction, and deletion

If you have sent information to Foculoom, you can request access, correction, or deletion by emailing support@foculoom.com. Requests are reviewed in light of applicable law, security needs, and recordkeeping obligations.

Retention

  • Support emails and attachments may be kept as long as reasonably needed to respond, troubleshoot, keep records, and meet legal obligations.
  • Routine website delivery and security logs are retained according to hosting, security, and operational needs.
  • When information is no longer reasonably needed, Foculoom deletes it or retains only what must be kept for legal, security, or recordkeeping reasons.

Children

This website is a general-audience site. If a future child-directed product introduces different privacy practices, that product will include its own updated privacy details before release.

California privacy disclosures

  • Foculoom does not use this site for cross-site advertising tracking.
  • Foculoom does not currently respond differently to browser Do Not Track signals.
  • California privacy requests can be sent to support@foculoom.com.

Changes to this policy

Foculoom may update this policy when the website, products, or legal requirements change. Material updates are posted on this page with a new review date.
