Privacy

Pre-launch website privacy notice.

This page describes the current website posture for Foculoom. It is not yet the final app privacy policy for future Foculoom releases.

App-specific privacy disclosures will be reviewed and published before the first app is submitted to the App Store.

What this site does today

  • Publishes public information about Foculoom and the current flagship product direction.
  • Does not provide app functionality, user accounts, or in-browser purchases.
  • Does not include client-side advertising, analytics, or social tracking scripts in this version of the site.

What to expect as the business launches

  • App-specific privacy notices will be posted at public URLs before launch.
  • Any future support contact or mailing-list flow will be documented before it goes live.
  • Privacy terms may change as product, platform, and legal requirements become more concrete.

Important context

Like most websites, the hosting platform may still process routine request information needed to deliver pages securely. If Foculoom later offers a support inbox, waitlist, or app account system, this notice will be updated alongside those features.
