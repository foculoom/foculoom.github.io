Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Foculoom LLC (DBA Foculoom) handles information on foculoom.com and in the public product information it chooses to publish on the site.

Effective date: March 12, 2026
Last reviewed: March 16, 2026. We update this page when the public site or public product scope changes materially.

What this policy covers

  • The public Foculoom website at foculoom.com.
  • The public product pages and public product information published on foculoom.com.
  • Support requests sent to support@foculoom.com.

What this policy does not do

  • It does not grant rights to content or trademarks beyond the public use of this site.
  • It does not override Apple's own privacy terms, App Store terms, or device-level settings.
  • It does not cover third-party sites you visit after leaving foculoom.com.

Website information

Foculoom does not run client-side advertising, analytics, or social tracking scripts on this website. Like most websites, the hosting and delivery providers that keep the site online may receive routine technical information such as IP address, browser details, and request information needed to deliver pages securely and keep the service available.

Public product information

Product pages on this site may describe in-development or pending-release products before their store listings are live. If a live app or game later introduces product-specific privacy details beyond what is covered here, that product page or live listing will link to the relevant information.

Support information

If you email support, Foculoom receives the information you include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, and the description of the problem. That information is used to respond to you, troubleshoot issues, and improve the quality of the product and public website.

Business contact details

  • Foculoom LLC (DBA Foculoom).
  • 2108 N ST STE N, Sacramento, CA 95816.
  • (310) 270-1918.
  • hello@foculoom.com for general company contact.

Categories of information we collect

  • Contact information you send directly, such as your email address.
  • Customer-support information you include in messages, such as device details, screenshots, app version information, and the description of a problem.
  • Technical request information processed to deliver the site, such as IP address, browser details, and routine server or hosting logs.

How information is used

  • To deliver and secure the public website.
  • To respond to support, accessibility, and privacy questions.
  • To troubleshoot product or website issues reported by users.
  • To meet legal obligations when required.

Sharing and selling

  • Foculoom does not sell personal information from this website or the current public app scope.
  • Foculoom does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Information may be handled by service providers that help deliver website hosting, email routing, or support operations.
  • Information may be disclosed if required to comply with law, protect users, or address abuse or fraud.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether to contact Foculoom by email.
  • You can stop using the website at any time.
  • If a Foculoom app requests device permissions in a later version, you can review and change those permissions in your Apple device settings.

Access, correction, and deletion requests

If you have sent information to Foculoom through support@foculoom.com, you can use that same address to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information you provided. Foculoom will review requests in light of applicable law and operational needs.

Retention and deletion handling

  • Support emails, attachments, and troubleshooting details may be kept as long as reasonably needed to respond, troubleshoot, keep business records, and meet legal obligations.
  • Routine website delivery and security logs are retained according to hosting, operational, and security needs.
  • When information is no longer reasonably needed, Foculoom deletes it or retains it only where recordkeeping, security, or legal obligations still require retention.

Children and general audience use

Foculoom products are being positioned for a general audience, not as child-directed services. If a future release changes how personal information from children is handled or introduces new data flows, this policy will be updated before that version goes live.

California Do Not Track disclosures

  • Foculoom does not currently respond differently to browser Do Not Track signals or similar mechanisms.
  • Foculoom does not use advertising or analytics scripts on this site to track users over time and across third-party websites.
  • Foculoom does not knowingly allow other parties to collect personal information through this site's own client-side advertising or analytics tools for cross-site tracking.

Policy changes and contact

Foculoom may update this policy when the website, the first-release app scope, or legal requirements materially change. Material changes will be posted on this page and reflected by updating the date shown above. If you have privacy questions or requests, contact support@foculoom.com.

Your California Privacy Rights

Foculoom does not disclose personal information collected through this site to third parties for their own direct marketing purposes. California privacy requests related to information you submitted through the site can be sent to support@foculoom.com. If Foculoom's direct-marketing disclosure practices materially change in the future, this page will be updated before those changes are described as part of the public website.
