Gameplay

Simple controls, endless fun.

One tap is all it takes. Jump over obstacles, grab coins, and see how far you can go.

Tap to jump

Touch anywhere to jump. The controls are simple enough for young players and responsive enough to keep the game fun.

Collect coins

Grab coins along the way. Save them up to unlock new characters from the character select screen.

Unlock characters

Three playable characters to choose from, each with its own look. Start with one and earn the rest through gameplay.

Chase your best score

Your high score and coin total are saved locally. No account needed — just play and try to beat your record.

Built for kids

Safe, simple, and local-first.

No accounts, no ads, no tracking in the current version. Everything stays on the device.

What the game does

  • Stores coins, high score, and preferences on the device.
  • Supports 6 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, and Japanese.
  • Works offline — no internet connection needed to play.
  • Runs on iPhone, iPad, Android, and web browsers.
  • Built with accessible defaults and clear structure.

What the game does not do

  • No account creation or sign-in in the current version.
  • No ads, analytics, or tracking in the current version.
  • No in-app purchases in the current version.
  • No network requests during gameplay.
