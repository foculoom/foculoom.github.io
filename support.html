Support

Need help?

Email support@foculoom.com for help with Focus & Tasks, the website, privacy, or accessibility. For general company, partnership, organization, educator, or public-interest inquiries, use hello@foculoom.com. Billing questions can still go to billing@foculoom.com.

Email support

  • support@foculoom.com Questions about Focus & Tasks, website issues, bug reports, privacy requests, and accessibility feedback.
  • Subject lines help Include the product name and a short summary of the issue when you can.
  • Scope This inbox is for product and website support.

Billing questions

  • billing@foculoom.com Questions about charges, purchases, or billing that need a developer response.
  • What to send Include the receipt email, purchase date, platform, and the issue you are seeing.
  • Store-specific steps When purchases are available, this page lists any platform billing or refund links that matter.

What to include

  • Device details Device model and operating system version.
  • App details App version, if the issue is inside Focus & Tasks.
  • Repro steps The steps you took right before the issue happened.
  • Screenshots Only when they help explain the issue without sharing sensitive information.
