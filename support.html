Support for Foculoom: Focus & Tasks

For help with Focus & Tasks or the public Foculoom website, contact Foculoom LLC at support@foculoom.com.

Email support Read privacy policy
When possible, include your device model, operating system version, app version, and the steps that led to the issue.

How to reach us

  • Email support@foculoom.com.
  • Foculoom LLC monitors this address for product questions, bug reports, accessibility issues, and problems with the public website.
  • Reference the app name and the problem you are reporting in the subject line when possible.

What to include

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • App version, if the issue is inside Focus & Tasks.
  • The steps you took right before the issue happened.
  • Any screenshots that help explain the problem without sharing sensitive information.

What this page covers

  • Support for Focus & Tasks.
  • Questions about the public Foculoom website.
  • Accessibility feedback and requests for public pages.
  • Privacy-related questions about the policy linked from this site.
