Support for Foculoom: Focus & Tasks

For help with Focus & Tasks, the public Foculoom website, privacy questions, or launch-era billing questions, contact Foculoom at support@foculoom.com or billing@foculoom.com.

Email support Email billing
When possible, include your device model, operating system version, app version, the steps that led to the issue, and any Apple receipt details if your question is about billing.

Product support inbox

  • support@foculoom.com Product questions, bug reports, privacy requests, accessibility feedback, and issues with the public website.
  • Subject lines help Reference the app name and the problem you are reporting when possible.
  • Scope This inbox is for product and website matters only. It is not an emergency or crisis service.
  • Advice limits Support responses do not provide medical, mental-health, legal, financial, or other professional advice.

Billing and App Store purchases

  • billing@foculoom.com Use this inbox for billing questions that still need a developer response.
  • What Apple handles When paid App Store tiers are live, subscription management, cancellation, and refund flows are handled by Apple.
  • What to send If you write about a charge or purchase state, include the Apple receipt email, purchase date, and the issue you are seeing.

What to include

  • Device details Device model and operating system version.
  • App details App version, if the issue is inside Focus & Tasks.
  • Repro steps The steps you took right before the issue happened.
  • Screenshots Only when they help explain the issue without sharing sensitive information.

When Apple handles the billing flow

  • Manage or cancel a subscription Use Apple's subscription settings when an App Store subscription is live. Open Apple's cancellation guide.
  • Request a refund Apple reviews App Store refund requests through reportaproblem.apple.com. Read Apple's refund guide.
  • Find the developer support link Apple routes users to the app developer's public support page from the App Store product page. Read Apple's support article.

These Apple links describe Apple's billing and account flows. Foculoom still handles product support, public support content, and developer-side troubleshooting.
